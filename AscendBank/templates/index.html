Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? Get answers to your common questions here.

  • 1. What types of accounts do you offer?

    We offer a variety of accounts to meet your needs, including checking accounts, savings accounts, credit cards, and loans. You can learn more about our accounts on our website or by speaking to a customer service representative.

  • 2. How do I open an account?

    To open an account, you can visit one of our branches or apply online. You will need to provide some basic information, such as your name, address, and Social Security number. You may also need to provide a deposit to open the account.

  • 3. What are your fees?

    We charge a variety of fees for our services, including monthly maintenance fees, transaction fees, and ATM fees. You can find a list of our fees on our website or by speaking to a customer service representative.

  • 4. How do I keep my account safe?

    Here are a number of things you can do to keep your account safe, such as:

    1) Creating a strong password and changing it regularly
    2) Enabling two-factor authentication
    3) Being careful about what information you share online
    4) Avoiding phishing scams
    5) Monitoring your account activity regularly

  • 5. What should I do if I lose my debit card?

    If you lose your debit card, you should contact your bank immediately to report it lost or stolen. Your bank will cancel the card and issue you a new one. You may also want to place a fraud alert on your account.
